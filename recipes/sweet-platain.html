Fried sweet platain

the fried sweet platain is a popular caribbean dish, is easy to preapre and fast to cook.

    Ingredients
  • Yellow platain
  • Olive oil
  • A pisk o salt
    Steps
  1. Pail and cut the sweet platain
  2. Set a pan on fire and pour the olvie oil
  3. When the oil get hot pour the platain
  4. Wait till platain get a golded color in order to take the platain off the pan
  5. Use some pisk salt onto your platains
  6. Lets eat !!!!!
