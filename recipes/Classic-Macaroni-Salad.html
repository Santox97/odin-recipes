Classic Macaroni Salad

This creamy macaroni salad with celery, onion, green pepper, carrot, and pimentos is easy to make and is the perfect crowd-pleasing dish to bring to your next picnic or potluck.

    Ingredients
  • Macaroni
  • Condiments
  • Sugar and vinegar
  • Seasonings
  • Vegetables
    Steps
  1. Cook the macaroni in salted water.
  2. Mix together mayonnaise, mustard, seasonings, vinegar, and sugar to taste.
  3. Stir in the macaroni and remaining ingredients. Chill in the refrigerator.
